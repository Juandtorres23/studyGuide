

CSS Units 

+ Absolute units
- Pixels (px)
-pt,cm,mm, in, etc

+ Percentages
- are mainly used for widths, and are pretty easy to understand
-Relative to their parent (except on height, where things get weird).

+Relative units
- Relative to font-size
	-em and rem
- Relative to the viewport
	-vw, vh, vmin, vmax
